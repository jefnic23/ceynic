Thanks so much for visiting!


All the paintings in my shop are original palette knife oil paintings on canvas and giclee art prints made from my original artwork. All the paintings in my shop are on gallery wrapped canvases with 1-1/2 inch wide edges that are staple free and painted white.


Paintings are shipped stretched on 1-1/2 inch wide stretcher bars (excluding paintings shipped outside U.S.). The paintings are carefully packed in bubble wrap and boxed in heavy duty boxes. Large paintings sent outside the continental United States are removed from the stretcher bars and shipped rolled in a tube, stretcher bars not included.


All my art prints are from my original oil paintings and are printed with certified archival inks on heavyweight matte giclee fine art paper.


I try to ship paintings the next day if possible, but some larger paintings may need an extra day or two to get carefully packed. The smallest paintings are usually sent by USPS Priority mail and larger paintings are sent by UPS or FedEx Ground.


If you have any questions about my artwork I'd be happy to answer your questions.


***International Customers*** Most paintings, except for the smallest ones, will have to be removed from stretcher bars and rolled in a tube, stretcher bars not included.
