Thanks for visiting!


The paintings you see here are oils, original and distinct, like snowflakes - no two are alike.


I am interested in color; its many hues, endless variations, and its ability to evoke a mood. It is in that spirit that I approach each canvas when I set to paint. Painting with just a palette knife on either linen or canvas affords me the greatest opportunity to blend colors resulting in the effects I like.


Purchased paintings are packaged with care and shipped as soon as possible but no longer than three days after purchasing.


Some of my original paintings have been reproduced using certified archival inks transferred onto heavyweight matte Giclee fine art paper. Sold as prints they are a favorite among the art collecting public.


For questions please contact me through the site; I will do my best to answer them. Thanks again for visiting.


—Tracey
